About Us

About Us

JL3 Services Limited

JL3 Services Limited is a registered Nigerian company based in Port Harcourt. We provide expert services in procurement, marine support, logistics, oilfield crew management, and general contracting.

Our offerings also include recruitment, credential verification, local and international procurement for the oil and gas sector—delivered on time and at competitive prices. With strong knowledge of shipping, handling, and customs clearance, we ensure smooth and timely delivery.

We supply essential oilfield consumables such as diesel, fresh water, lubricants, safety gear, welding materials, as well as providing transport, hotel bookings, and meet-and-greet services.

At JL3 Services, we help clients maintain a reliable and efficient supply chain.

Vision

To be Nigeria’s top marine, procurement, and logistics company, focused on customer satisfaction and service excellence.

Mission

To use knowledge and technology to deliver fast, reliable and valuable services, empowering our clients to succeed in their operations.

Core Values
  • Integrity: We are honest, transparent and accountable.
  • Quality: We provide the right solutions that meet high standards.
  • Timeliness: We are committed to on-time delivery.
  • Efficiency: We work smart to save time and cost.
  • Safety & Compliance: We prioritize safety and adhere strictly to industry regulations.
Operating Principle

We are guided by integrity, precision, and reliability. We deliver timely, safe, and effective solutions that meet client needs and exceed expectations. Our focus is on building trust through consistent performance and value.
