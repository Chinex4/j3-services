OUR FOCUS AREAS

Buying House, Marine Services, Oilfield Crew Management & Solid Minerals

Buying House: Our Procurement Services serve local and International Oil & Gas Industry. As a buying house, we act as a mediator between buyers and suppliers. We assist in finding, selecting, and managing the procurement of goods or services.

Marine Transportation, Vessel Management: Our marine transportation and vessel management services play a crucial role in the offshore industry, facilitating the movement of personnel, supplies, and equipment.

Oil Field Crew Management: Our oilfield crew management encompasses the processes of selecting, training, scheduling, and supervising the workforce operating in oil and gas operations. This critical function ensures the safe, efficient, and compliant operations, from recruiting and onboarding to ongoing monitoring and training. Key areas include crew planning and scheduling, compliance and certification management, and health, safety, and well-being (HSE) management.

Solid Mineral: Our solid mineral services supply bentonite and barite to the drilling, oil & gas as well as manufacturing industries. We are expanding our domestic supply and looking for export opportunities.
